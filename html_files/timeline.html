The World of Rutheria tends to be divided into three main eras: The Era of Creation, The Era of Progression, and the Era of Recession. During these times, the world of Rutheria experienced significant changes and developments. From wars fought between gods and demons, to mortal socities that reached near divinity powers. The histroy of Rutheria is filled with such vast mysteries, that to this day, a need to explore what was once lost tends to overpower even basic needs at times.
